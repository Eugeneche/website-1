</ul>

<p>We process billing data such as name, surname, address, email, phone number, and photos/videos taken during the service.</p>
<ul>
  <li><strong>Legal basis:</strong> Article 6(1)(b) and (c) GDPR – contract performance and legal obligations.</li>
  <li><strong>Retention:</strong> During the service and up to 10 years afterward (accounting and tax purposes).</li>

<p>If you are a customer and did not opt out during your purchase, we may use your email to send updates and offers.</p>

</ul>

<p>Data may be accessed by our contracted processors (e.g., accounting, hosting, email service providers). All processors are within the EU and subject to proper data protection agreements.</p>

<p>We use cookies to ensure website functionality, analyze traffic, and personalize content.</p>

</ul>

<p>According to GDPR, you have the right to:</p>
